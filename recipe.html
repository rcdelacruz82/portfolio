Grilled Pork Tenderloin and Mango Salad

Juicy grilled pork tenderloin combined with fresh mangoes to create a colourful, refreshing & easy salad.

Prep Time:
5 minutes

Cook Time:
20 minutes

Serving Size:
4

In Partnership with

Ingredients you'll need

1 1kg (approx.) Maple Leaf Pork Pork Tenderloin
1 ½ tbsp vegetable oil
2 tbsp Great Valu chili powder
Salt
Pepper
1 454gr Pkg Your Fresh Market Spring Mix
¼ cup Great Valu Honey Dijon Vinaigrette
2 ½ cup Chopped fresh Mango
Cilantro – if desired

Directions

  1. Preheat one side of the grill to high heat. Place pork onto a large platter and season with oil, chili powder and salt and pepper. Thoroughly rub seasoning onto pork tenderloin.
  2. Grill pork 2 to 3 minutes per side.
  3. Line a baking sheet with tin foil and set onto unheated side of grill. Place pork onto baking sheet and close lid. Cook pork on residual heat for 45 or 55 minutes of until internal temperature of 165 degrees is reached.
  4. Place greens in a large bowl, add mango and toss with salad dressing.
  5. Allow pork to rest for about 5 minutes and slice.
  6. Plate salad and serve pork over top. Garnish with cilantro if using.
